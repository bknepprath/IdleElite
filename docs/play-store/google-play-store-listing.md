## Store Listing Copy

### App Name

Idle Elite

### Short Description

Train five idle skills, earn upgrades, and become an absurdly elite hero.

Character count: 72 / 80.

### Full Description

Become the world's most determined idle elitist.

Idle Elite is a handmade idle RPG about training every useful job in town. Fight strange little battles, sneak through thieving jobs, build your way upward, chop wood, fish for rewards, and turn every short check-in into progress.

Each skill has its own level, XP, stamina, and activity ladder. When one skill runs low, jump to another. Earn cash, gather resources, unlock better actions, and build a hero who gets stronger even when you step away.

Features:

- Train Fight, Thieving, Build, Woodcutting, and Fishing.
- Rotate between skill-specific stamina pools.
- Earn XP, cash, logs, fish, and upgrade resources.
- Unlock stronger actions as each skill levels up.
- Enjoy a simple portrait layout built for quick mobile sessions.
- Come back to claim idle progress and keep climbing.

Idle Elite keeps the charm of an old-school Flash idle game and rebuilds it for mobile: simple, readable, funny, and progression-heavy.

No forced reflexes. No complicated controls. Pick a job, start training, and become elite one tiny task at a time.
